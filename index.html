Trivia Game

Instructions

This is an advanced trivia game. Once you press the start button, a timer and a question will pop up and begin counting down. You must answer the question in the alloted time. The game will give you show you the correct answer when you either, answer the question, or the timer runs out. The game will give you the next question in a few seconds without any input. After all the questions have been answered or missed, the game will tally your score and give you the option to try again!
